Custom Web Application
A simple work flow system can save you millions, not costing you. Let's explore how.
Our 4 quadrants of a successful project implementation.



Understanding
Understand your challenges and goals is our top priority.
We work closely with you to analyse your functional flow and come out with a solution best catering to your organizational needs.
We will produce a Functional Specifications Document which detail out all work flows within the application. A skeleton interface, designs and user experience will be presented before the commencement of the implementation phase.
Implementing
We adopt Agile Development methodology and are in constant re-evaluating the direction of the project to allow all stakeholders to calibrate. Ensuring the success of the project.
We have our own proprietary software framework which is constantly being enhanced for more than 8 years. The framework formed the base for all the application that we build.
Together with version controls, matured libraries, UX centric development and our skilful talent, we create successful application that is easy to use and affordable.
Deploying
We test the application vigorously against all scenarios before our initial deployment.
With our organized development structure and framework, we are able to deploy the application frequently and accurately with updates on each iteration.
Our QA and development team work very closely to ensure each iteration meets the requirements.
We implement daily incremental remote backup (latest 30 days) of your database and all uploaded files ensuring your business continuity.
Improving
We constantly evaluate and improve the application in the light of its efficiency, effectiveness and flexibility.
We do not believe in a stagnant application. Business processes may change once a system is in place and this will uncover improved methods to handle different case scenarios.
Our framework and structure ensures the application we build is extensible and able to be integrated with third party system through system APIs.
