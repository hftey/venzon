E-Commerce Systems

E-Commerce System consists primarily of the distributing, buying, selling, marketing, and servicing of products or services over electronic systems such as the Internet. E-Commerce generated sales worth US $12.2 billion in 2003 and today, this is not only a crucial part of a company's long-term corporate strategy in cost containment, but also in maintaining and winning market share in a borderless, global marketplace.

Our E-Commerce System with osCommerce technology has all the functionality for your online global marketplace strategy. Features include:
  • Full administrative management including Add/Edit/Remove products, manufaturers, customers.
  • Flexible products and categories organization.
  • Product promotional tools.
  • Customer relation modules.
  • Fast and simple search and advance search features for customers.
